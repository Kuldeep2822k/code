About The Project

The Indian Meal Calculator was developed to address the specific nutritional needs of those who enjoy Indian cuisine. Traditional nutrition apps often lack comprehensive data on Indian foods, making it difficult for users to accurately track their nutrition.

Our solution combines a user-friendly interface with powerful features like image recognition and barcode scanning, all while maintaining a focus on Indian dishes and ingredients.

The application is built with modern web technologies and requires no backend server, making it easy to deploy and scale.
